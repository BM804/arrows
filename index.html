Play Arrows – Puzzle Escape: Free Web Arrow Game

Arrows – Puzzle Escape is a free web arrow game built for instant online game play — no download, no install, just open the page and play online in your browser. Every board is filled with arrow-shaped paths pointing in different directions. Your goal is simple: tap a path to send it sliding off the grid in the direction its arrowhead points. The catch is that the path only escapes cleanly if the runway ahead of it is empty. Clip another path along the way and you lose a life.

As you progress through the levels, boards grow denser and the puzzles demand more careful planning.
